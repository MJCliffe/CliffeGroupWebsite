We are a materials chemistry group working at the University of Nottingham.

In January 2026 the group will be moving to the University of Cambridge, Department of Materials Science and Metallurgy!

Please get in touch if you are interested in joining us in this new chapter for the group, whether as a project student, graduate student or postdoctoral fellow, please get in touch to explore possible funding options.

Our research focuses on exploring metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) as quantum materials. We are interested in how we can control and characterise disorder in MOFs to generate new and unusual magnetic and electronic behaviour. Our work is generously supported by the UKRI Frontiers ERC Guarantee Funding Project "DISCO-MOF".
