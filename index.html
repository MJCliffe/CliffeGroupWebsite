We are the Functional Inorganic and Hybrid Materials (FIHM) group working at the University of Cambridge, Department of Materials Science and Metallurgy.

Our research focuses on exploring metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) as quantum materials. We are interested in how we can design MOFs with new and unusual magnetic and electronic behaviour. Our work is generously supported by the UKRI Frontiers ERC Guarantee Funding Project "DISCO-MOF".

If you are interested in joining us, whether as a project student, graduate student or postdoctoral fellow, please get in touch with a CV and to explore possible funding options.
