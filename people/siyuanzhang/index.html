Siyuan Zhang

Mon, Jan 1, 0001

sz521@cam.ac.uk

Siyuan is currently a MPhil student in Micro and Nanotechnology Enterprise. He completed his BSc in Chemistry at University College London (UCL). His research focuses on two-dimensional van der Waals metal–organic frameworks (vdW MOFs), with particular interest in exploring magnetic properties. With a background in chemistry and materials research, Siyuan is interested in the design and understanding of low-dimensional functional materials. Outside the lab, Siyuan enjoys watching football, travelling, video gaming, and staying active through a wide range of sports.
